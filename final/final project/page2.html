France

Overview

France is known for its romantic cities, art, and food. Paris alone has famous landmarks like the Eiffel Tower and the Louvre Museum. Info inspired by Paris Tourist Office.

Paris Highlights

  • Eiffel Tower – iconic iron tower with city views.
  • Louvre Museum – huge art museum with the Mona Lisa.
  • Seine River – boat tours and river walks.

Art & Museums

Visitors often spend hours inside the Louvre and other museums in Paris. You can see paintings, sculptures, and historical objects from many time periods. [source]
