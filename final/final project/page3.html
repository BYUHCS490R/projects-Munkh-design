Brazil

Overview

Brazil is famous for beautiful beaches, Carnival parades, and the Amazon rainforest. It is the largest country in South America with many different landscapes. Info inspired by Visit Brasil.

Rio de Janeiro

Rio is known for Christ the Redeemer statue, Sugarloaf Mountain, and Copacabana Beach. Many visitors enjoy city views from the hills around Rio. [source]

Festivals & Nature

  • Carnival – parades, costumes, and music in major cities.
  • Beaches – warm weather and long coastlines.
  • Amazon rainforest – rich biodiversity and river tours.
