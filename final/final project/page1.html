Japan

Overview

Japan is a country where modern technology and ancient traditions exist side by side. You can take high-speed trains, visit quiet temples, and enjoy city lights all in one trip. Info inspired by Japan National Tourism.

Top Places to Visit

  • Tokyo – shopping, anime, food, and city views.
  • Kyoto – temples, shrines, and traditional tea houses.
  • Osaka – street food and a more relaxed city feel.

Mt. Fuji & Nature

Mt. Fuji is the highest mountain in Japan and a national symbol. Many visitors enjoy seeing it from nearby lakes or hiking part of the trail. [source]
